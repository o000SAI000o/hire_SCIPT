# Employer-Focused ATS Design
## Project Overview & Workflow Summary

### 1. Project Purpose
A comprehensive Applicant Tracking System (ATS) designed to automate the hiring pipeline, from job creation to final selection, with a focus on AI-driven candidate scoring (ATS Score) and structured interview management.

### 2. User Roles
*   **Employer (Admin):** Manages jobs, views all applicants, configures ATS weights, manages the team, and oversees the hiring funnel.
*   **Panel (Interviewer):** Focused on conducting interviews, viewing assigned slots, and providing structured evaluations/feedback.
*   **Candidate:** Applies for jobs via shareable links and participates in assignments and interviews.

### 3. Core Modules & Flow

#### A. Job Management
*   **Creation:** Employers create job postings with specific details (Title, Dept, Type, Salary).
*   **ATS Configuration:** Employers define "Weightage" for Skills (e.g., 40%), Education (30%), and Projects (30%).
*   **Verification:** Employers set a "Threshold" (e.g., 70%). Candidates scoring above this are automatically flagged as "Recommended".
*   **Shareable Links:** Each job has a unique link for candidates to apply directly.

#### B. Application & ATS Scoring
*   **Application:** Candidates submit their details, skills, and resume.
*   **Automatic Scoring:** The system calculates an ATS Score (0-100) based on the job's defined weightages for Skills match, Education (CGPA/Degree), and Project experience.
*   **Stats Tracking:** Real-time tracking of Total Applicants, ATS Pass/Fail, and Funnel progression.

#### C. Applicants Management (The "Funnel")
*   **Filtering:** Advanced filtering by Stage (Applied, Shortlisted, etc.), College, and Search.
*   **Stages:** Candidates move through structured stages:
    1.  **Applied:** Initial state.
    2.  **ATS Shortlisted:** Passed the ATS score criteria.
    3.  **Assignment Sent:** Coding/Design task sent to the candidate.
    4.  **Assignment Passed:** Candidate met the passing score.
    5.  **Interviews (L1, L2, HR):** Scheduled rounds with panel members.
    6.  **Selected/Rejected:** Final decision.
*   **Bulk Actions:** Ability to move multiple candidates to the next stage or reject them in one click.

#### D. Assignment System
*   **Creation:** Employers create assignment templates (Coding, Data Analysis, etc.) with passing scores and durations.
*   **Integration:** Bulk-send assignments to shortlisted candidates directly from the Applicants page.

#### E. Interview & Evaluation
*   **Scheduling:** Integrated calendar to check panel availability and schedule rounds (L1, L2, HR).
*   **Evaluation:** Panel members provide scores for Technical, Communication, Problem Solving, and Culture Fit.
*   **Decision:** Structured "Recommended" or "Rejected" decisions with detailed notes.

#### F. Team Management
*   **Roles:** Management of HR Admins and Technical Panel members.
*   **Performance:** Tracking interviews conducted by each team member.

### 4. Technical Stack
*   **Frontend:** React (TSX), Vite, Tailwind CSS, Shadcn/UI, Lucide Iconography.
*   **Backend:** Node.js, Express, Prisma ORM.
*   **Database:** SQLite (dev.db) for responsive and localized storage.
*   **State Management:** TanStack Query (React Query) for efficient data fetching and caching.
